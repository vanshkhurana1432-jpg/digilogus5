About Us

Digilogous 12.0 is the flagship technology and innovation event hosted by Rukmini Devi Public School, Pitampura. Now in its twelfth edition, it brings together students, schools, and young innovators for a day built around curiosity, competition, and creativity.

Details

Expect a mix of tech competitions, coding challenges, robotics demos, quizzes, and creative showcases across categories for different age groups. Full schedule, categories, and rulebooks will be published here closer to the event.
